Mysterium: Murder Mystery Game

Case 1 - Death at the Orchid Gala

Clue-Based Deduction: At the glamorous Orchid Gala, world-renowned botanist Dr. Leena Ramaswamy is found dead. Was it a tragic accident or a calculated murder? Analyze witness statements, examine the crime scene, and uncover hidden motives. Can you solve the case before the orchids wilt?

Case 2 – The Silence in Server Room 6

Data-Based Deduction: At Neuronexus, a cutting-edge AI research lab, Dr. Elina Rao is discovered dead in Server Room 6. You’ll need to sift through digital data breadcrumbs to find the truth. Was she in the wrong place at the wrong time—or was she targeted? Follow the data to solve the case.
